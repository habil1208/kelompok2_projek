About TheGhibliCafe

Our cafe is inspired by the magical worlds created by Studio Ghibli. We aim to create a cozy environment where fans can enjoy themed drinks, desserts, and a touch of anime magic. Our team is passionate about bringing the enchanting stories of Ghibli to life through our menu and ambiance.
